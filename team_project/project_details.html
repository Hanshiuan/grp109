planthaus

What the project is

This is an online plant shop for our mock brand: "Planthaus"

Planthaus has a "sleek, modern, and boutique feel." The name suggesting a "trendy, minimalist, and creative approach" According to Mumtaas, the creator behind the name for the shop.

Project purpose

The Planthaus website will contain mock products, purchases, a front page carousel, a plant care page, profile, cart, and more!

How will it work?

We will have a website with barebones features initially, and slowly growing it as we delve into JS and other functions. Starting with 10 total products

Example: Product pages and cart, but no functional "add/remove from cart"
