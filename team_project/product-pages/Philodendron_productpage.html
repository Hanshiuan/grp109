About this product


The Philodendron Brandtianum also known as the Silver Leaf Philodendron. This unique tropical plant has deep olive green heart-shaped leaves with prominent silver or iridescent gray patches that give this plant the name Silver-leaf. Another common name for this variety is Brandi Philodendron. As an epiphyte, a hemiepiphyte, and a terrestrial plant, Brandi can grow in most households. But growth rate and leaf color will vary greatly depending on soil type, sunlight, temperature.
